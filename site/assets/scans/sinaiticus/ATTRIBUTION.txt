NOTE ON JOHN 1:1-18 FOLIO IMAGES
----------------------------------
The specific folio page showing John 1:1-18 (Quire 80, Folio 1, Recto)
is viewable online at codexsinaiticus.org but uses a tile-based zoom
viewer that does not expose direct image download URLs. The high-resolution
page images are served through a dynamic tile system.

To obtain the specific John 1 folio image:
  1. Visit: https://codexsinaiticus.org/en/manuscript.aspx?book=36&chapter=1&lid=en&side=r&verse=1
  2. Use browser screenshot or the site's own tools
  3. Or contact the Codex Sinaiticus Project for academic use

The images in this directory are general Codex Sinaiticus reference images
showing the manuscript's characteristic four-column layout, uncial script,
and overall appearance. They serve as visual reference for the translation
work on John 1:1-18.

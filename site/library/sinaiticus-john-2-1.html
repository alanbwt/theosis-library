§2:1–5 · Codex Sinaiticus, John 2:1–5
Manuscript
Greek

καὶ τῇ ἡμέρᾳ τῇ τρίτῃ γάμος ἐγένετο ἐν Κανὰ τῆς Γαλιλαίας

English

On the third day there was a wedding at Cana in Galilee, and the mother of Jesus was there. Jesus also was invited to the wedding with his disciples. When the wine ran out, the mother of Jesus said to him, ‘They have no wine.’ And Jesus said to her, ‘Woman, what does this have to do with me? My hour has not yet come.’

Translator's Notes

  1. §2:4: The address “Woman” (γύναι) is not disrespectful in Greek but is notably formal for a son addressing his mother. The phrase “my hour has not yet come” introduces the Johannine theme of Jesus’ “hour,” which reaches fulfillment at the crucifixion.

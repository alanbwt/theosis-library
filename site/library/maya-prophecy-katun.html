Maya Prophecy: The Katun Wheel

Maya Prophecy: The Katun Wheel
Chilam Balam Scribes, c. 17th-18th century AD
Chilam Balam manuscripts

Introduction

Maya cyclical prophecy: each 20-year katun brings specific events, and history repeats when the same katun returns.

Translation

§1 · maya-prophecy-katun §1

When Katun 13 Ahau returns, there will be great suffering. When Katun 8 Ahau returns, there will be abundance.

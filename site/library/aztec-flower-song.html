Flower and Song: Aztec Philosophical Poetry

Flower and Song: Aztec Philosophical Poetry
Aztec Elders, c. 16th century AD
Cantares Mexicanos; Romances de los Señores

Introduction

Aztec poets ask the ultimate questions: is anything real? Do we truly live on earth? Only flower and song endure.

Nahuatl original and English translation.
§1 · CM §1
Nahuatl

In tlaltipac. Zan achica ye nican. Tel ca chalchihuitl. No xamani. No teocuitlatl in tlapani.

English

On earth. We are here only briefly. Even jade shatters. Even gold breaks apart.

§2 · CM §2
Nahuatl

Ma zan in xochitl. Ma zan in cuicatl.

English

Let there be only flowers. Let there be only song.

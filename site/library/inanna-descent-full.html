Translation

        
        

          §1 · inanna-descent-full
          
From the great above she opened her ear to the great below. From the great above the goddess Inanna opened her ear to the great below. She abandoned heaven and earth and descended. At each of seven gates, she was stripped of one of her seven powers. Naked and bowed low, she entered the throne room of her sister Ereshkigal, queen of the dead. Ereshkigal turned the eye of death upon her, and Inanna became a corpse hung from a hook.

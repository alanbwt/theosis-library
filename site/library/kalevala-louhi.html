Elias Lonnrot, 1802-1884 AD
SKS; oral sources

Introduction

The sorceress Louhi steals the sun and moon, plunging the world into darkness until Vainamoinen tricks her.

Translation

§1 · kalevala-louhi §1

Louhi, the gap-toothed mistress of Pohjola, stole the sun from the sky and the moon from its place.

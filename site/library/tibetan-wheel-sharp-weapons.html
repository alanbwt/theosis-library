The Wheel of Sharp Weapons

        
Atisha, 982-1054 AD

        
Kadampa tradition

        
      


      

        
Introduction

        
A fierce mind-training text: every suffering you experience is the result of your own past actions. Turn the weapon of karma back on the ego.

Translation

        
        

          §1 · tibetan-wheel-sharp-weapons
          
When everything goes wrong in our lives, the sharp weapon of karma is circling back. All the harm we have done to others returns to us. Therefore, from now on, let us take upon ourselves the suffering of all beings.

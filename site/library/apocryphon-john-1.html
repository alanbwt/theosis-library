§1 · AJ §2

The Monad is a monarchy with nothing above it. It is he who exists as God and Father of everything.

And Sophia of the Epinoia, being an aeon, conceived a thought from herself — she wanted to bring forth a likeness out of herself without the consent of the Spirit.

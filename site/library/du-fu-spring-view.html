Spring View
Du Fu, 712-770 AD
Complete Tang Poems

Introduction

Du Fu surveys the ruins of the capital during the An Lushan Rebellion: the nation is shattered, but mountains and rivers remain.

§1 · CV
Chinese

國破山河在，城春草木深。感時花濺淚，恨別鳥驚心。

English

The nation shattered, mountains and rivers remain. In the city in spring, grass and trees grow deep. Moved by the times, flowers bring tears. Hating separation, birds startle the heart.

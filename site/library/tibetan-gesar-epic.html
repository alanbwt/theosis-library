Epic of King Gesar: The Warrior from Heaven
Attributed to Padmasambhava, 8th century AD
Oral tradition; various block prints

Introduction

The longest epic in the world (over 1 million verses in some versions): the divine hero Gesar descends to earth to defeat demons.

Translation

§1 · tibetan-gesar-epic §1

Gesar descended from heaven to the land of Ling. He had been sent by the gods to subdue the demons of the four directions.

Introduction

Tablet 6 of the Epic of Gilgamesh: The goddess Ishtar proposes marriage to Gilgamesh; he rejects her. She sends the Bull of Heaven.

Translation

§1 · SBV 6

Gilgamesh said: 'What could I offer you? Your lovers have found you like a brazier that smolders in the cold.'

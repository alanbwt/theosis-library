Odu Ifa: The Sixteen Sacred Chapters

Odu Ifa: The Sixteen Sacred Chapters
Yoruba Oral Tradition, pre-modern
Oral tradition; Bascom transcription

Introduction

The 256 odu of Ifa represent a complete philosophical system — each chapter containing narratives, proverbs, and prescriptions that address every possible situation in human life. The system is binary (each odu is a combination of single and double marks, like a 4-bit code), making it one of the oldest known binary systems. Ifa priests memorize thousands of verses across all 256 chapters.

Yoruba original and English translation.
§1 · Ogbe Meji
Yoruba

Orunmila lo difa fun araiye gbogbo. O ni ki won rubo ki ire ba won.

English

Orunmila cast divination for all people of the world. He said they should sacrifice so that good fortune would come to them.

§2 · Oyeku Meji
Yoruba

Ayanmo ni o nmu ni lo. Ohun ti a ba se ni aye, o tele ni lo si orun.

English

It is destiny that drives us. What we do in this world follows us to heaven.

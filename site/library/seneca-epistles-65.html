Latin original and English translation.
§65.2 · Ep. 65.2
Latin

Quinque ergo causae sunt, ut Plato dicit: id ex quo, id a quo, id in quo, id ad quod, id propter quod.

English

There are therefore five causes, as Plato says: that from which, that by which, that in which, that toward which, and that on account of which.

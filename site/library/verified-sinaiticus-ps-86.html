            §86.1-5 · Psalms 86:1-5
            

              
              
              
            

          


          
          

            
Manuscript

            
            
            
          

          

          

            
Greek (Codex Sinaiticus) / English (KJV)

                    
1τοιϲ ϋϊοιϲ κορε ψαλμοϲ ωδηϲ οι θεμελιοι αυτου εν τοιϲ ορεϲι τοιϲ αγιοιϲ 2αγαπα κϲ ταϲ πυλαϲ ϲειων ϋπερ παντα τα ϲκηνωματα ϊακωβ 3δεδοξαϲμενα ελαληθη περι ϲου η πολιϲ του θυ διαψαλμα 4μνηϲθηϲομαι ρααβ και βαβυλωνοϲ τοιϲ γινωϲκουϲι με και ϊδου αλλοφυλοι και τυροϲ και λαοϲ αιθιοπων 5μητηρ ϲειων ερει ανθρωποϲ και ανθρωποϲ εγενηθη εν αυτη και αυτοϲ εθεμελιωϲεν αυτην ο ϋψιϲτοϲ

          


          

            
English

                    
1Bow down thine ear, O LORD, hear me: for I
am poor and needy. 2Preserve my soul; for I
am holy: O thou my God, save thy servant that trusteth in thee. 3Be merciful unto me, O Lord: for I cry unto thee daily. 4Rejoice the soul of thy servant: for unto thee, O Lord, do I lift up my soul. 5For thou, Lord,
art good, and ready to forgive; and plenteous in mercy unto all them that call upon thee.

          

        

        
        


          

            §86.6-10 · Psalms 86:6-10
            

              
              
              
            

          


          
          

            
Manuscript

            
            
            
          

          

          

            
Greek (Codex Sinaiticus) / English (KJV)

                    
6κϲ διηγηϲεται εν γραφη λαων και αρχοντων τουτων των γεγενημενων εν αυτη διαψαλμα 7ωϲ ευφραινομενων παντων η κατοικια εν ϲοι

          


          

            
English

                    
6Give ear, O LORD, unto my prayer; and attend to the voice of my supplications. 7In the day of my trouble I will call upon thee: for thou wilt answer me. 8Among the gods
there is none like unto thee, O Lord; neither
are there any works like unto thy works. 9All nations whom thou hast made shall come and worship before thee, O Lord; and shall glorify thy name. 10For thou
art great, and doest wondrous things: thou
art God alone.

English

                    
11Teach me thy way, O LORD; I will walk in thy truth: unite my heart to fear thy name. 12I will praise thee, O Lord my God, with all my heart: and I will glorify thy name for evermore. 13For great
is thy mercy toward me: and thou hast delivered my soul from the lowest hell. 14O God, the proud are risen against me, and the assemblies of violent
men have sought after my soul; and have not set thee before them. 15But thou, O Lord,
art a God full of compassion, and gracious, longsuffering, and plenteous in mercy and truth.

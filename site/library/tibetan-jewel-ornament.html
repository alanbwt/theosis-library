Translation

        
        

          §1 · tibetan-jewel-ornament
          
All sentient beings possess buddha-nature. The cause of buddhahood is within every being. The condition is a spiritual friend. The method is the instructions of the spiritual friend. The result is the perfect body of a buddha.

Introduction

The coldest political philosophy ever written: virtue is useless, only laws and punishments can govern men.

§49 · HF §49
Chinese

上古競於道德，中世逐於智謀，當今爭於氣力。

English

In high antiquity men competed in virtue. In the middle ages they pursued in cleverness. Today they struggle with force.

Commentary on the Yijing: Principle and Material Force
Cheng Yi, 1033-1107 AD
Song dynasty manuscripts

Introduction

Cheng Yi distinguishes principle (li) from material force (qi) — the foundation of Neo-Confucian metaphysics.

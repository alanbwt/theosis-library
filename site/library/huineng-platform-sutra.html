Platform Sutra: Sudden Enlightenment

Platform Sutra: Sudden Enlightenment
Huineng, 638-713 AD
Dunhuang manuscript; Zongbao edition

Introduction

The Sixth Patriarch of Chan Buddhism teaches that enlightenment is sudden, not gradual — mind itself is Buddha.

Chinese original and English translation.
§1 · PS §1
Chinese

菩提本無樹，明鏡亦非台。本來無一物，何處惹塵埃。

English

Bodhi originally has no tree. The mirror also has no stand. Originally there is not a single thing — where could dust alight?

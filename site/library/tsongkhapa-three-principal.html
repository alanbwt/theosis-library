Three Principal Aspects of the Path

Three Principal Aspects of the Path
Tsongkhapa, 1357-1419 AD
Gelug monastic manuscripts

Introduction

Tsongkhapa distills the entire Buddhist path into three essentials: renunciation, bodhicitta, and the correct view of emptiness.

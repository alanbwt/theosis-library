Introduction

The Shona creation narrative: Mwari, the high god, creates the first people and speaks to them through the oracle of Matonjeni.

Translation

§1 · shona-creation §1

Mwari created the sky and the earth. He created the first man and woman and placed them in the garden of Matopos.

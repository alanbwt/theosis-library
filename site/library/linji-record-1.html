§Record 1 · Linji Lu
Manuscript
Greek

師示衆云、赤肉団上有一無位真人

English

The Master ascended the hall and said: “On your lump of red flesh there sits a true person of no rank who is always going in and out of the face of every one of you. Those who have not yet confirmed this—look, look!” A monk came forward and asked: “What is the true person of no rank?” The Master seized the monk and said: “Speak! Speak!” The monk hesitated. The Master pushed him away: “The true person of no rank—what a piece of dried shit!”

§Record 2 · Linji Lu
Manuscript
Greek

逢佛殺佛、逢祖殺祖

English

The Master said: “Followers of the Way, if you want insight into the dharma as it is, just don’t be taken in by the deluded views of others. Whatever you encounter, slay it at once. On meeting a buddha, slay the buddha. On meeting a patriarch, slay the patriarch. Then for the first time you will gain emancipation.”

Translator's Notes

  1. §Record 1: The “true person of no rank” draws on Daoist language but transforms it. For Linji, this true person is not a metaphysical entity but the living, present awareness in each person—which cannot be grasped conceptually. The monk’s hesitation proves he is looking for an object where there is only activity.

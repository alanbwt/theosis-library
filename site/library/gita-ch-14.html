Introduction

Chapter 14 of the Bhagavad Gita: All of nature consists of three qualities: sattva (goodness), rajas (passion), tamas (ignorance).

Translation

§1 · BG 14

Sattva binds through attachment to happiness and knowledge. Rajas binds through attachment to action. Tamas binds through negligence and sleep.

Hatata: An Ethiopian Rationalist Philosophy

Hatata: An Ethiopian Rationalist Philosophy
Zara Yaqob (philosopher), 1599-1692 AD
Various Ge'ez manuscripts

Introduction

An Ethiopian philosopher independently develops rational theology: rejecting all religions as human inventions while affirming God through reason alone.

Translation

§1 · zara-yaqob-hatata §1

All religions are equal in their falsity. But God exists, and this can be known by reason alone.

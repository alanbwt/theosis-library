Enneads VI.8: On Free Will and the Will of the One

Enneads VI.8: On Free Will and the Will of the One
Plotinus, 204-270 AD
Various manuscripts

Introduction

Is the One free? It is beyond freedom — it is what it wills, and wills what it is. There is no gap between its being and its willing.

Translation

§1 · plotinus-6-8

The One does not deliberate. It is its own will. There is no distance between what it is and what it chooses.

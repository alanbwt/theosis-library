Introduction

        
This entry presents Tablet V — The Combat with Humbaba of the Epic of Gilgamesh — the oldest major work of literature, composed in Akkadian cuneiform around 2100-1200 BCE.
  1. Tablet photograph: Cuneiform tablet photo from CDLI (artifact P397688), showing the actual clay tablet with the wedge-shaped script.
  2. Original text: Akkadian transliteration from Langdon's scholarly edition (public domain).
  3. English translation: Langdon's English rendering (Project Gutenberg #18897, public domain).

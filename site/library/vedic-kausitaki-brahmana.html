Translation

        
        

          §1 · vedic-kausitaki-brahmana
          
After death, the soul of the wise reaches the world of Brahma. He is greeted by Brahma himself: 'Who are you?' The soul says: 'I am you, the eternal Self.' Brahma says: 'Whatever I am, you are. Welcome.'

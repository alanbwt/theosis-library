Plague Prayers of Mursili II

        
Mursili II, c. 1321-1295 BCE

        
Bogazkoy tablets (CTH 378)

        
      


      

        
Introduction

        
A Hittite king confesses his father's sins and begs the gods to end a plague.

Translation

        
        

          §1 · hittite-plague-prayers
          
O Storm God of Hatti, my lord! My father sinned and transgressed against your word. But I have not sinned. Yet the sin of the father falls upon the son. I have confessed before you. Take pity on me and drive the plague out of the land of Hatti.

Interior Castle: The Seventh Mansion

Interior Castle: The Seventh Mansion
Teresa of Avila, 1515-1582 AD
Autograph manuscript

Introduction

Teresa's map of the soul: seven mansions leading to the center where God dwells. The seventh is spiritual marriage.

Translation

§1 · theresa-interior-castle-7 §1

In this seventh mansion, the soul enters a new life. It is as if a small stream enters the sea and cannot be separated from it again.

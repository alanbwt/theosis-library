Translation

        
        

          §1 · maya-hero-twins-complete
          
The Lords of Xibalba invited the twins to the underworld. They passed every test: the House of Darkness, the House of Cold, the House of Jaguars, the House of Fire, the House of Bats. They were killed and ground to powder, but the powder was thrown in the river and from the river they were reborn. They danced the dance of death and resurrection. They cut themselves to pieces and made themselves whole. Then they cut the Lords of Death to pieces — and did not restore them. The twins ascended: one became the sun, the other the moon.

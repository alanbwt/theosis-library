Slavic Creation: The World Tree

Slavic Creation: The World Tree
Slavic Oral Tradition, pre-Christian era
Reconstructed from folklore; Afanasyev

Introduction

The Slavic cosmos organized around the World Tree: its crown reaches heaven, its roots descend to the underworld.

§1 · WT §1
Old Church Slavonic

Стоит дуб от земли до неба. Корни его в преисподней, ветви его в небесех.

English

An oak stands from earth to sky. Its roots are in the underworld, its branches in the heavens.

The Dialogue of Pessimism

        
Sumerian Scribes, c. 2100-1900 BCE

        
British Museum tablets

        
      


      

        
Introduction

        
The most nihilistic text from the ancient world — a master and slave agree with every proposal and its opposite.

Translation

        
        

          §1 · dialogue-pessimism
          
'Slave, I will do a good deed.' 'Do, sir.' 'No, I will not.' 'Do not, sir. Go to the ancient ruins. See the skulls of the great and the lowly. Which is the benefactor?'

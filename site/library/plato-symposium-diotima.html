Symposium: Diotima's Ladder of Love

Symposium: Diotima's Ladder of Love
Plato, c. 428-348 BCE
Various manuscripts

Introduction

Diotima teaches Socrates the ascent of love: from one beautiful body to all bodies, to the beauty of souls, to Beauty itself.

Translation

§1 · plato-symposium-diotima

Beginning from beautiful bodies, one ascends to beautiful souls, to beautiful practices, to beautiful forms of knowledge, until at last one arrives at Beauty itself.

§44.3-4 · Y. 44.3-4
Avestan

tat θβā pərəsā ərəš mōi vaocā ahurā: kas hātąm xratūš paourvīm dadāt?

English

This I ask you, tell me truly, Lord: who was the first father of Truth? Who established the course of the sun and stars?

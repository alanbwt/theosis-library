Enneads II.9: Against the Gnostics

Enneads II.9: Against the Gnostics
Plotinus, 204-270 AD
Various manuscripts

Introduction

Plotinus attacks the Gnostics: they insult the cosmos by calling it evil, when it is the best possible image of the divine.

Satires X: The Vanity of Human Wishes

Satires X: The Vanity of Human Wishes
Juvenal, c. 55-130 AD
Various manuscripts

Introduction

What should we pray for? Not wealth, power, beauty, or long life — all lead to ruin. Pray only for a sound mind in a sound body.

§1 · juvenal-satires-10 §1

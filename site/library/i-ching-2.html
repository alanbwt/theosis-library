§Hexagram 2: Kun (The Receptive) · I Ching, Hexagram 2
Manuscript
Greek

坤。元亨。利牡馬之貞。地勢坤。君子以厚德載物

English

The Receptive brings about sublime success, furthering through the perseverance of a mare. The earth’s condition is receptive devotion. Thus the superior person who has breadth of character carries the outer world. Straight, square, great. Without purpose, yet nothing remains unfurthered.

Translator's Notes

  1. §Hexagram 2: The image of the mare—strong yet yielding—expresses the paradox of receptive power. In Chinese cosmology, yin is not passive weakness but the nurturing force that gives form and substance to yang’s creative impulse. The pair Qian-Kun (hexagrams 1-2) maps the fundamental polarity of existence.

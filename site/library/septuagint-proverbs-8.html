Proverbs 8:22-31: Wisdom Before Creation

Παροιμίαι
Solomon (attributed), traditional
Septuagint (LXX)

Introduction

Personified Wisdom speaks: she was present before creation, delighting in the works of God.

Greek original and English translation.
§8.22-25 · Prov. 8.22-25
Greek

Κύριος ἔκτισέν με ἀρχὴν ὁδῶν αὐτοῦ εἰς ἔργα αὐτοῦ· πρὸ τοῦ αἰῶνος ἐθεμελίωσέν μέ ἐν ἀρχῇ.

English

The Lord created me as the beginning of his ways for his works; before the age he established me, in the beginning.

§8.30-31 · Prov. 8.30-31
Greek

ἤμην παρ᾿ αὐτῷ ἁρμόζουσα... εὐφραινομένη ἐν οἰκουμένῃ γῆς.

English

I was beside him as a craftsman... rejoicing in the inhabited world.

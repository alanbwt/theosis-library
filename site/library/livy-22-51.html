Translation

        
        

          §1 · livy-22-51
          
On the day after Cannae, when the magnitude of the disaster was known, the senate decreed that no woman should be in the streets, that mourning should be limited to thirty days, and that the praetor should appoint a day for sacrifices to appease the gods.

Translation

        
        

          §1 · zhuangzi-ch-29
          
Confucius was a friend of Liuxia Ji, whose younger brother was Robber Zhi. Robber Zhi had nine thousand followers. They marauded their way through the empire, attacking and raiding the various lords. They tunneled through walls and broke into rooms, drove off other people's cattle and horses, abducted their wives and daughters.

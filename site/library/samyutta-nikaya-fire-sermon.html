Samyutta Nikaya: The Fire Sermon

Samyutta Nikaya: The Fire Sermon
Gautama Buddha, c. 563-483 BCE
Pali Canon

Introduction

The Buddha declares everything is burning — with passion, aversion, and delusion. T.S. Eliot quoted this in The Waste Land.

Translation

§35.28 · SN 35.28

Bhikkhus, all is burning. And what is the all that is burning? The eye is burning.

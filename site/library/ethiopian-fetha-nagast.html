Introduction

        
The legal code of the Ethiopian Empire — combining Roman law, Mosaic law, and canon law into one system that governed Ethiopia for 700 years.

Translation

        
        

          §1 · ethiopian-fetha-nagast
          
This is the law of the kings, derived from the apostolic canons and the laws of the emperors, for the governance of the Christian kingdom of Ethiopia.

            §3.1 · Od. 3
            

              
              
              
            

          


          

          

            
Greek

            
Ἠέλιος δ᾿ ἀνόρουσε, λιπὼν περικαλλέα λίμνην.

          


          

            
English

            
The sun rose from the beautiful lake. Telemachus arrived at Pylos and found Nestor sacrificing to Poseidon. The old king told him of the Greeks' homecomings — of Agamemnon's murder by Clytemnestra and Aegisthus, of Menelaus's wanderings. But of Odysseus he knew nothing. He sent Telemachus onward to Sparta.

Introduction

The sage Vainamoinen builds the kantele from a pike's jawbone and plays so beautifully that all of nature weeps.

Soitti vanhat, soitti nuoret. Ei ollut sitä metsässä, jok' ei kuulohon tulisi.

English

He played for old and young. There was nothing in the forest that did not come to listen.

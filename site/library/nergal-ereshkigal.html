Nergal and Ereshkigal: Love in the Underworld
Sumerian Scribes, c. 2100-1900 BCE
Amarna tablets; Sultantepe tablets

Introduction

The sky god Nergal descends to the underworld, seduces Queen Ereshkigal, and becomes king of the dead.

Translation

§1 · nergal-ereshkigal §1

Nergal descended through the seven gates. When he saw Ereshkigal, his heart was seized with desire.

Introduction

        
A seven-day performance epic from the Niger Delta — a boy raised by his grandmother to avenge his father's murder fights progressively more powerful enemies.

Translation

        
        

          §1 · african-ozidi-saga
          
Ozidi's grandmother said: 'Your father was murdered by the elders. When you are grown, you must avenge him.' She taught him every fighting art. She prepared medicines of power. When Ozidi was ready, he went to the town of his enemies.

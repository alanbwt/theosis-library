Kalevala: The Tragedy of Kullervo

Kalevala: The Tragedy of Kullervo
Elias Lonnrot, 1802-1884 AD
SKS; oral sources

Introduction

The slave Kullervo, abused since birth, unknowingly commits incest and kills himself — the darkest story in Finnish mythology. Tolkien based Turin Turambar on this.

Translation

§1 · kalevala-kullervo §1

Kullervo, son of Kalervo, drew his sword and asked it: 'Are you willing to eat guilty flesh?' The sword answered: 'Why would I not?'

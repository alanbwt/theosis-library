Introduction

        
The Maya calendar system — the most sophisticated in the ancient world. The Long Count measures time from a creation date of August 11, 3114 BCE.

Translation

        
        

          §1 · maya-calendar-round
          
The current creation began on 4 Ahau 8 Cumku — the day the gods set the three stones of the cosmic hearth and raised the sky. Before this creation, there were three previous worlds, each destroyed. We live in the fourth world.

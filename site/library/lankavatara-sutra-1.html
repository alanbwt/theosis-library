Lankavatara Sutra: Mind-Only

Lankavatara Sutra: Mind-Only
Gautama Buddha, c. 563-483 BCE
Chinese and Tibetan translations

Introduction

The key text of the Mind-Only school: all phenomena are projections of consciousness, and liberation is recognizing this.

§1 · LS §1
Sanskrit

svacittadṛśyamātraṃ hi bālair gṛhyate grāhyagrāhakataḥ

English

What fools grasp as subject and object is nothing but the manifestation of their own mind.

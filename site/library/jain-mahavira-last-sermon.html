Mahavira's Last Sermon: The Final Teaching

Mahavira's Last Sermon: The Final Teaching
Jain Acharyas, c. 6th-3rd century BCE
Shvetambara tradition

Introduction

Mahavira's final words before achieving moksha: complete non-attachment, complete non-violence, complete liberation.

Translation

§1 · jain-mahavira-last-sermon §1

Endure all things with equanimity. Do not harbor anger. Do not boast. Do not deceive. Free yourself from all bonds.

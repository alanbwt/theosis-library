Analects 9.5: Heaven's Culture Cannot Be Destroyed

Analects 9.5: Heaven's Culture Cannot Be Destroyed
Confucius, 551-479 BCE
Dingzhou bamboo

Introduction

When threatened, Confucius declares that if Heaven intended this culture to survive, no one can destroy it.

Translation

§1 · analects-9-5 §1

If Heaven intends this culture to be destroyed, then I, a future dead man, shall not be able to share in it. But if Heaven does not intend it to be destroyed, what can the people of Kuang do to me?

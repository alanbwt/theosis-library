Bhagavad Gita 3.19-26: Action Without Attachment
Attributed to Vyasa, legendary
Critical Edition (BORI)

Introduction

Krishna teaches that even the wise must act — but without clinging to results.

Translation

§1 · gita-3-19 §1

Therefore, without attachment, always perform the work that has to be done; for by performing action without attachment, one attains the Supreme.

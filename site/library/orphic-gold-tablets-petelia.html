Introduction

The Orphic gold tablets (c. 4th century BCE) were buried with initiates as instructions for navigating the underworld. The Petelia tablet, found in southern Italy, directs the soul to avoid the spring of Forgetfulness and drink instead from the Lake of Memory.

§Full text · Petelia gold tablet (OF 476)
Manuscript
Greek

Εὑρήσεις δ’ Ἀΐδαο δόμων ἐπ’ ἀριστερὰ κρήνην, πὰρ δ’ αὐτῇ λευκὴν ἑστηκυῖαν κυπάρισσον· Γῆς παῖς εἰμι καὶ Οὐρανοῦ ἀστερόεντος

English

You will find in the halls of Hades a spring on the left, and standing beside it a white cypress. Do not approach this spring. You will find another, from the Lake of Memory, cold water flowing forth, and there are guardians before it. Say: “I am a child of Earth and starry Heaven; but my race is of Heaven alone. I am parched with thirst and I perish; give me quickly the cold water flowing from the Lake of Memory.”

Translator's Notes

  1. §Full text: The formula “I am a child of Earth and starry Heaven” (Γῆς παῖς εἰμι καὶ Οὐρανοῦ ἀστερόεντος) asserts dual origin: mortal body from Earth (Ge) and divine soul from Heaven (Ouranos). This is among the earliest surviving claims of innate human divinity in the Greek tradition.

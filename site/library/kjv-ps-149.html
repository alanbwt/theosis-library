Translation

        
        

          §149.1-5 · Psalms 149:1-5
          
1Praise ye the LORD. Sing unto the LORD a new song,
and his praise in the congregation of saints. 2Let Israel rejoice in him that made him: let the children of Zion be joyful in their King. 3Let them praise his name in the dance: let them sing praises unto him with the timbrel and harp. 4For the LORD taketh pleasure in his people: he will beautify the meek with salvation. 5Let the saints be joyful in glory: let them sing aloud upon their beds.

        

        
        

          §149.6-9 · Psalms 149:6-9
          
6Let the high
praises of God
be in their mouth, and a twoedged sword in their hand; 7To execute vengeance upon the heathen,
and punishments upon the people; 8To bind their kings with chains, and their nobles with fetters of iron; 9To execute upon them the judgment written: this honour have all his saints. Praise ye the LORD.

Mulamadhyamakakarika: The Root Verses of the Middle Way

Mulamadhyamakakarika: The Root Verses of the Middle Way
Nagarjuna (attributed), c. 150-250 AD
Tibetan and Chinese canonical translations

Introduction

Nagarjuna's foundational text of Mahayana philosophy: nothing has inherent existence, everything arises dependently.

Sanskrit original and English translation.
§1.1 · MMK 1.1
Sanskrit

na svato nāpi parato na dvābhyāṃ nāpy ahetutaḥ | utpannā jātu vidyante bhāvāḥ kvacana kecana

English

Neither from itself nor from another, nor from both, nor without cause, does anything anywhere ever arise.

§24.18 · MMK 24.18
Sanskrit

yaḥ pratītyasamutpādaḥ śūnyatāṃ tāṃ pracakṣmahe

English

That which is dependent arising, we call emptiness.

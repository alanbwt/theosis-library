Republic VI: The Allegory of the Sun

Republic VI: The Allegory of the Sun
Plato, c. 428-348 BCE
Various manuscripts

Introduction

As the sun illuminates the visible world, so the Form of the Good illuminates the intelligible world.

Translation

§1 · plato-republic-allegory-sun

As the sun gives both visibility and growth to the objects of sight, so the Good gives both intelligibility and being to the objects of thought.

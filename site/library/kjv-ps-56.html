Translation

        
        

          §56.1-5 · Psalms 56:1-5
          
1Be merciful unto me, O God: for man would swallow me up; he fighting daily oppresseth me. 2Mine enemies would daily swallow
me up: for
they be
 many that fight against me, O thou most High. 3What time I am afraid, I will trust in thee. 4In God I will praise his word, in God I have put my trust; I will not fear what flesh can do unto me. 5Every day they wrest my words: all their thoughts
are against me for evil.

        

        
        

          §56.6-10 · Psalms 56:6-10
          
6They gather themselves together, they hide themselves, they mark my steps, when they wait for my soul. 7Shall they escape by iniquity? in
thine anger cast down the people, O God. 8Thou tellest my wanderings: put thou my tears into thy bottle:
are they not in thy book? 9When I cry
unto thee, then shall mine enemies turn back: this I know; for God
is for me. 10In God will I praise
his word: in the LORD will I praise
his word.

        

        
        

          §56.11-13 · Psalms 56:11-13
          
11In God have I put my trust: I will not be afraid what man can do unto me. 12Thy vows
are upon me, O God: I will render praises unto thee. 13For thou hast delivered my soul from death:
wilt not
thou deliver my feet from falling, that I may walk before God in the light of the living?

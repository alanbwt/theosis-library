Old Irish original and English translation.
§1 · Suidigud §1
Old Irish

Cóiced Connacht siar, fis. Cóiced Ulad thuaid, cathugud. Cóiced Laigen soir, borrfad. Cóiced Muman deas, ceol.

English

Connacht in the west: knowledge. Ulster in the north: battle. Leinster in the east: prosperity. Munster in the south: music.

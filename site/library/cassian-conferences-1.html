Conferences: On the Goal of the Monk
John Cassian, c. 360-435 AD
PL 49

Introduction

The Egyptian monk Abba Moses teaches: the immediate goal is purity of heart; the ultimate goal is the kingdom of heaven.

Translation

§1 · cassian-conferences-1 §1

The end of our profession is the kingdom of God. But the immediate aim is purity of heart, without which it is impossible to reach that end.

§1 · DYXD
Chinese

花間一壺酒，獨酌無相親。舉杯邀明月，對影成三人。

English

Among the flowers, a jug of wine — I drink alone, no friend at hand. I raise my cup to invite the bright moon; with my shadow we make three.

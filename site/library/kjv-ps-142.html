Translation

        
        

          §142.1-5 · Psalms 142:1-5
          
1I cried unto the LORD with my voice; with my voice unto the LORD did I make my supplication. 2I poured out my complaint before him; I shewed before him my trouble. 3When my spirit was overwhelmed within me, then thou knewest my path. In the way wherein I walked have they privily laid a snare for me. 4I looked on
my right hand, and beheld, but
there was
 no man that would know me: refuge failed me; no man cared for my soul. 5I cried unto thee, O LORD: I said, Thou
art my refuge
and my portion in the land of the living.

        

        
        

          §142.6-7 · Psalms 142:6-7
          
6Attend unto my cry; for I am brought very low: deliver me from my persecutors; for they are stronger than I. 7Bring my soul out of prison, that I may praise thy name: the righteous shall compass me about; for thou shalt deal bountifully with me.

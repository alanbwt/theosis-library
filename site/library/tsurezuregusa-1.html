A monk's reflections on beauty, impermanence, and the art of living — the Japanese Montaigne.

Japanese original and English translation.
§1 · TG §1
Japanese

つれづれなるままに、日くらし硯にむかひて。

English

In the tedium of idle hours, I sit all day facing my inkstone.

§7 · TG §7
Japanese

あだし野の露消ゆる時なく、鳥部山の煙立ち去らでのみ住み果つる習ひならば、いかにもののあはれもなからん。

English

If the dew on the moor of Adashino never vanished, if the smoke on Mount Toribe never cleared, and man lingered on forever — how little the beauty of things would move us.

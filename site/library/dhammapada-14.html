Dhammapada 14: The Awakened One
Gautama Buddha, c. 563-483 BCE
Pali Canon, Khuddaka Nikaya

Introduction

The Buddha on what it means to be truly awake: seeing the world as it actually is.

Pali original and English translation.
§179 · Dhp. 179
Pali

yassa jitaṃ nāvajīyati jitam assa no yāti koci loke | taṃ buddham anantagocaraṃ apadaṃ kena padena nessatha

English

He whose victory cannot be undone, whose victory no one in the world can reach — by what track can you lead that trackless, awakened, infinite-ranging one?

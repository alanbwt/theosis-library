Elegies: Trust No One

Elegies: Trust No One
Theognis, c. 6th century BCE
Codex Mutinensis

Introduction

The elegies of Theognis preserve the worldview of the archaic Greek aristocracy in its twilight: a world where old values are being overturned, new money corrupts old blood, and the poet can trust no one. These poems were used as a school text throughout antiquity.

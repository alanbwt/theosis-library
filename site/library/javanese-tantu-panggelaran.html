Javanese Court Poets, c. 9th-14th century AD
Palm-leaf manuscripts

Introduction

The gods move Mount Meru from India to Java, flattening the island in the process — the Javanese creation myth.

Translation

§1 · javanese-tantu-panggelaran §1

The gods carried Mount Meru from Jambudvipa to Java. When they set it down, the island was pressed flat.

Shantideva, c. 685-763 AD

Introduction

Shantideva's masterwork on compassion: the aspiration to attain enlightenment for the sake of all sentient beings.

Sanskrit original and English translation.
§1.10 · BCA 1.10
Sanskrit

yāvad ākāśam avatișṭhate yāvac ca jīvasattvadhātuḥ | tāvad aham avatișṭheyaṃ lokaduḥkhavyapohane

English

As long as space endures, as long as sentient beings remain, may I too remain to dispel the suffering of the world.

Translation

        
        

          §1 · tibetan-book-kadam
          
When I arrived in Tibet, I was asked: 'What is the essence of all the teachings?' I said: 'The essence is this: refrain from all harmful actions, perform only virtuous ones, and tame your own mind. This is the teaching of all the buddhas.'

Laurentian Codex (1377)

Introduction

The Primary Chronicle (Tale of Bygone Years) is the foundational text of Russian, Ukrainian, and Belarusian historiography. Compiled by the monk Nestor at the Caves Monastery in Kiev around 1113, it traces the origin of the Slavic peoples and records the famous 'invitation' of 862 — when the quarreling Slavic and Finnic tribes supposedly asked the Varangian Rus to come establish order.

§1 · PVL §1
Old Church Slavonic

Се повести времяньных лет, откуду есть пошла Руская земля.

English

These are the stories of bygone years, whence the Russian land had its beginning.

§2 · PVL §862
Old Church Slavonic

Земля наша велика и обильна, а наряда в ней нет. Да пойдете княжить и владети нами.

English

Our land is great and rich, but there is no order in it. Come and rule over us.

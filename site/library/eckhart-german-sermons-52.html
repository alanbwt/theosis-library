German Sermon 52: Blessed Are the Poor in Spirit
Meister Eckhart, c. 1260-1328 AD
Various manuscripts

Introduction

The most radical sermon ever preached: true poverty means wanting nothing, knowing nothing, having nothing — not even God.

Translation

§1 · eckhart-german-sermons-52 §1

A person should be so poor that he is not even a place where God could work. As long as there is a place, there is still something.

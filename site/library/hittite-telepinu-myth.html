Myth of Telepinu: The Vanishing God

        
Myth of Telepinu: The Vanishing God

        
Hittite Scribes, c. 1650-1180 BCE

        
Bogazkoy tablets (CTH 324)

        
      


      

        
Introduction

        
The fertility god vanishes in rage and all life withers.

Translation

        
        

          §1 · hittite-telepinu-myth
          
Telepinu flew into a rage. He put his right shoe on his left foot. Mist seized the windows. The sheep rejected their lambs. Barley and wheat no longer grew. A bee found him and stung him awake. A ritual of purification calmed his anger.

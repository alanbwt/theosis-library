Mozi: Universal Love
Mozi, c. 470-391 BCE
Daozang edition

Introduction

Mozi's radical argument: if everyone loved others as they love themselves, war, theft, and hatred would cease.

Chinese original and English translation.
§14 · MZ §14
Chinese

天下之人皆相愛，強不執弱，眾不劫寡，富不侮貧，貴不敖賤。

English

If everyone in the world loved one another, the strong would not overpower the weak, the many would not oppress the few, the rich would not insult the poor, the noble would not be contemptuous of the humble.

Fifth Theological Oration: On the Holy Spirit

Fifth Theological Oration: On the Holy Spirit
Gregory of Nazianzus, 329-390 AD
PG 36

Introduction

Gregory completes the doctrine of the Trinity: the Spirit proceeds from the Father, not as generated but as 'breathed forth.'

Translation

§1 · gregory-naz-theological-orations-5 §1

The Spirit proceeds from the Father, not by generation but by procession. What the difference is, tell me, and I will tell you what the generation and procession of God are.

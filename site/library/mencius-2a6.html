Chinese original and English translation.
§2A.6 · Mengzi 2A.6
Chinese

惻隱之心，仁之端也；羞惡之心，義之端也；辭讓之心，禮之端也；是非之心，智之端也。

English

The feeling of compassion is the beginning of humaneness; the feeling of shame is the beginning of righteousness; the feeling of deference is the beginning of propriety; the feeling of right and wrong is the beginning of wisdom.

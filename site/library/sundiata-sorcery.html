Sundiata: The Sorcerer's Defeat
Manding Griots, c. 13th century AD
Niane transcription

Introduction

Sundiata confronts Soumaoro the sorcerer-king, who is invulnerable to weapons — and defeats him with a rooster's spur tipped with the spirit of his ancestors.

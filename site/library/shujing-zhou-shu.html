Translation

        
        

          §1 · shujing-zhou-shu
          
The king spoke to this effect: 'Feudal prince, my younger brother, little one Feng. Your greatly distinguished father, the king Wen, was able to illustrate his virtue and be careful in punishments. He did not dare to insult the friendless and widows.'

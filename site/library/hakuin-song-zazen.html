Song of Zazen

Song of Zazen
Hakuin Ekaku, 1686-1769 AD
Rinzai Zen school manuscripts

Introduction

Hakuin's verse on the identity of samsara and nirvana: this very place is the lotus land, this very body the Buddha.

Translation

§1 · hakuin-song-zazen §1

All beings are from the very beginning Buddhas. It is like water and ice: apart from water, no ice.
